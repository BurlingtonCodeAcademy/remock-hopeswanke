The One that Got Away

An Intro

Last summer, even as he talked about facing jail time Steve couldn’t stop looking for that bird. “There’s that couple yellow-crowned night herons,” he thought, looking out his living-room window. “They roost in that chinaberry tree.” He rested his eyes on the blue-gray birds. ---Enormous flocks of American avocets, willets, sander lings, dowitchers and plovers feed by his perch. Steve built his life amid a clump of trees so he could always be watching birds; he layed a bird blind. Birds are his obsession and his profession.
